What Exactly Is Roof Maxx?

So what exactly is Roof Maxx? In short, it's a clear coating sprayed on asphalt shingle roofs to help protect and prolong their life.

  • Roof Maxx claims to penetrate into the shingles to form a durable sealant barrier. This prevents leaks, reduces roof damage from weathering and the elements, and reflects more sun rays to lower attic temperatures.
  • The main ingredient is an acrylic polymer and various conditioners and plasticizers. Roof Maxx dries to form a flexible, breathable coating, allowing for expansion and contraction.
  • Roof Maxx is sprayed on by certified contractors, typically taking 1-2 days for an average-sized roof. The coating is reapplied every 5-7 years to maintain protection. Roof Maxx states their product can extend the life of shingles up to 15 years.
  • The cost varies but typically runs between $0.75 to $1.25 per square foot, making Roof Maxx a budget-friendly option compared to re-roofing. However, the long-term cost may become similar once reapplication fees are considered.

The idea of a magical elixir to instantly revive your aging roof may seem too good to be true. But for some, Roof Maxx could buy extra time before needing to re-roof. As with any product, research to determine if it's the right solution for your home.

How to Apply Roof Maxx and What to Expect

Preparation

Before applying Roof Maxx, clean your asphalt shingle roof to remove any dirt or debris. Pressure wash the roof if there is built-up grime. Allow the roof to dry completely, which usually takes 2-3 days. Roof Maxx requires a clean, dry surface to properly adhere.

Application

A professional roofer applies Roof Maxx to ensure even, comprehensive coverage. The product is sprayed on using a specialized pump sprayer. Two coats are typically required, waiting 2-3 hours between applications. The coating dries quickly to form a durable finish within 30-60 minutes.

What to Expect

Once applied and dried, Roof Maxx provides a number of benefits to asphalt shingles. It helps prevent cracks, splits, and curling while protecting against weathering and UV damage. The coating is flexible, moving with the shingles, and waterproof, preventing leaks. By using Roof Maxx, you can increase the lifespan of your shingles by up to 15 years. However, Roof Maxx requires reapplication every 5-10 years to maintain protection for the best results.

The coating is nearly invisible once dry, maintaining the appearance of your roof. Roof Maxx does not significantly change the color or texture of shingles. Roof Maxx has protective abilities that may lower your homeowner's insurance premiums. Please note that it does not offer any extra fire resistance.
