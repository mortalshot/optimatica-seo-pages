Knowing What to Look for in a Roofing Company

So you're looking for a roofing company. Where do you even begin? Well, shopping around and researching to find a reliable and trustworthy company is important. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Company's credentials: Do they have certifications, awards, reviews, or memberships with trade organizations? They should be. This demonstrates that the company possesses the know-how, proficiency, and experience you seek in a roofing contractor.
  • Warranties: Any good roofing company should have some warranty on their workmanship and materials. Ask about this before signing any contract with them.

At Directorii, we thoroughly vet all contractors, examining their certifications, awards, and reviews and conducting background checks on the owners. This ensures that they're reputable and have no history of questionable business practices.

Additional Factors to Consider When Choosing a Roofing Company

  • Reaction time: In urgent situations, you'll want a prompt response from the company. Observe how they handle communication on the interview stage. Do they seem overly aggressive or pressure you to sign a contract, leaving you uneasy? Trust your instincts and opt for another reliable company in your vicinity.
  • Specialization: Investigate the types of roofing materials companies specialize in and the properties they typically work on. For instance, do they focus on residential or commercial buildings or both? Are they experienced with shingles, metal, tile, or flat roofing systems? Understanding their expertise will help you choose a roofing company that suits your needs and preferences.

Assessing Services Offered by Roofing Companies

As you research potential companies, examine their offerings, such as roof inspections, gutter repairs, roof maintenance, re-roofing, siding installation, and more. Opting for a company that provides comprehensive care is ideal because, in the event of damage, storms, or improper installation, it's better to have the same roofers who initially installed the roof handle any issues that may arise.

Key Services

It's crucial to select a roofing company with specific key services that answer all of your needs:

  1. Roof Inspections: After storm damage or purchasing a new home, an inspection determines whether your roof needs repair or replacement.
  2. Emergency Repairs: During extreme weather conditions like hurricanes or a hailstorm, roofs may need emergency repairs to prevent further damage from occurring.
  3. Gutter Repairs: An essential piece for preventing water damage, gutters should be repaired as soon as possible if there is damage or clogging from leaves and other debris.
  4. Roof Maintenance: To keep your roof looking new and in top condition, it needs regular maintenance, depending on the material used.
  5. Re-Roofs: A complete re-roof job replaces the existing materials on top of your home with new ones for maximum strength and longevity; this is often done with metal roofs or shingles for better weather protection and insulation against noise and temperature extremes.
